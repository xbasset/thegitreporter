# Code Of Conduct

TheGitReporter is a public-interest reporting project. We welcome critique,
questions, corrections, and disagreement when they are grounded in evidence and
offered in good faith.

Please:

- challenge claims with sources;
- separate evidence from interpretation;
- be direct without being hostile;
- assume readers and contributors have different levels of technical context;
- avoid harassment, personal attacks, spam, and bad-faith repetition.

Maintainers may moderate Issues, Discussions, and Pull Requests to keep the
project useful, factual, and welcoming.
